DSCR Loan Details

  • • Qualification: Based on property Debt Service Coverage Ratio (Income vs Expense).
  • • No DTI: No personal income or debt-to-income checks. No tax returns required.
  • • Terms: 30-Year Fixed or Interest-Only options.
  • • LTV: Up to 80% on purchases, 75% on cash-out refi.
  • • Entity: Close in an LLC, Corp, or Trust.

Bridge & Fix Details

  • • Purpose: Quick acquisition and funding for property renovations.
  • • Speed: Approval in 48 hours. Closing in as little as 7-10 days.
  • • Coverage: Up to 90% Loan-to-Cost (LTC) and 100% of rehab costs.
  • • Duration: 12 to 24 month terms with interest-only payments.
  • • Exit: Designed for resale (flip) or refinancing into a long-term DSCR.

Equity Access Details

  • • Recycle Capital: Use a Cash-Out Refi to pull initial investment out for new deals.
  • • Leverage: Access up to 75% - 80% of current appraised value.
  • • Flexibility: No restrictions on how you use the cash proceeds.
  • • Tax Benefit: Cash-out proceeds are a loan, not considered taxable income.
